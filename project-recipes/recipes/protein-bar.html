A tasty, yet healthy treat to crave your sweet tooth!

Description

Easier to make, better than the rest!

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Sweetener of choice
  • Coconut flour
  • Zero sugar dark chocolate

Steps (Ten servings)

  1. Take two scoops of protein powder and place in a mixing bowl
  2. Add a cup of greek yoghurt
  3. Add sweetener
  4. Add a cup of coconut flour
  5. Mix till smooth and doughy
  6. Place in baking tray
  7. Cut into ten equal rectangular pieces
  8. Place 20grams of dark chocolate in a mixing bowl with sweetener and melt
  9. Drizzle onto bars and place in refrigerator
